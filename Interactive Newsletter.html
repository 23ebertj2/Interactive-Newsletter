What We Are Learning

Unit 2: Polynomial & Rational Functions

This week we are diving deep into the limiting behavior of rational functions. We will move beyond simple graphing to understand the underlying mechanics of "End Behavior" and "Discontinuities".

  • Vertical Asymptotes: Identifying infinite discontinuities where the denominator equals zero.
  • Holes (Removable Discontinuities): What happens when a factor cancels out?
  • End Behavior Models: Analyzing the ratio of leading terms to predict horizontal or slant asymptotes.
